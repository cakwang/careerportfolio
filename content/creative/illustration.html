Approach

My illustration process begins with a deep understanding of the concept or message that needs to be communicated. I research thoroughly and develop sketches that explore different visual approaches before refining the final illustration. Whether working digitally or traditionally, I focus on creating images that are both aesthetically engaging and informationally clear.

Client Work

I've created illustrations for educational publishers, research institutions, and corporate clients. My work has been featured in textbooks, learning applications, research publications, and corporate training materials. Each project is approached with careful attention to the client's communication goals and audience needs.
