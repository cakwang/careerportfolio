Photography

Photography allows me to capture moments, perspectives, and details that might otherwise go unnoticed. My photographic work focuses primarily on documentary, architectural, and environmental subjects, often exploring the intersection of human design and natural environments.

Approach

My photographic approach emphasizes careful observation and composition to reveal meaningful details and relationships. I'm particularly interested in how photography can be used as a research and documentation tool, capturing visual data that informs educational and design processes.

Applications

My photography has been used in educational publications, research documentation, institutional communications, and as visual resources for learning materials. I also incorporate photography into my own research and design process as a way to gather visual information and inspiration.

Equipment

I work with both digital and film photography, selecting the appropriate tools for each project. My equipment includes:

  • Digital mirrorless system with various lenses
  • Medium format film camera for select projects
  • Macro photography equipment
  • Portable lighting setup for documentary work
