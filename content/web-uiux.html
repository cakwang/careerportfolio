Design Philosophy

My design approach is guided by several core principles:

  • User-Centered: Prioritizing user needs and experiences in every design decision
  • Accessibility: Creating interfaces that are usable by people with diverse abilities
  • Simplicity: Reducing complexity without sacrificing functionality
  • Consistency: Maintaining coherent design patterns throughout the user experience
